CARAT WEIGHT

The weight of gemstones is measured in a unit called carats (cts.). There are 5 carats in 1 gram.

Please Note: The carat weight listed on the website for pairs are the combined total weight of the two gemstones.

To gauge the size of a gemstone (visual appearance) it is always better to look at the millimeter measurements of the gemstone as opposed to the carat weight.
